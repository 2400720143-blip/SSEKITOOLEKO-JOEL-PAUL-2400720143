AFRICAN LITERATURE

This is a vibrant online platform dedicated to celebrate rich literary heritage of Africa. Discover,read and explore books, authors and voices from Africa and across the continent. African Literatre refers to the body of literary works produced by writers of African descent, both on the continent and in in the diaspora.It reflects the diverse histories,cultures,languages and experiences of Africa's peoples,spanning oral traditions,poetry,drama,novels,folktales and modern prose.Rooted in rich oral storytelling,myths and folklore, African literature has evolved into a powerful medium for expressing identity,cultural heritage,resistence and social change.
